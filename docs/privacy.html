Privacy Policy

No Budget Slate · Last updated 18 May 2026

No Budget Slate is a digital film/video slate. It is designed to do one thing on your device and nothing else.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that gather information about you.

Data stored on your device

The slate field values you enter (title, scene, take, audio file, and audio channel labels) are saved locally on your device so they persist between launches. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Network

The app makes no network requests. It works fully offline.

Microphone & other permissions

The app does not use the microphone, camera, location, contacts, or any other protected data. (A microphone usage description is present in the app only because a system audio library links the relevant API; the microphone is never accessed or activated.)

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
